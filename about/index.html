    Audley Burnett - Acupuncture

    Audley qualified in Five Element Acupuncture in 1992 after studying for three years at the College of Traditional Chinese Acupuncture, Leamington Spa, under Professor “J.R” Worsley (1923-2003).

    He took graduate studies at the College of Integrated Chinese Medicine, Reading.

    Audley has a History Master’s Degree from Cambridge University and pursued further studies at the London School of Economics.

    For eighteen years Audley was a member of the Traditional Acupuncture Centre in Waterloo, Europe’s oldest and largest group practice and a centre of excellence devoted to providing Leamington style Five Element Acupuncture.

    Further studies have included

    • Zero Balancing bodywork and functional integration.
    • Cranial Sacral Therapy with Mike Boxhall of the Karuna Institute.
    • Tui Na (Chinese medical massage) apprenticed learning with Dr Li He.
    • Auricular Acupuncture in the Nada Protocol (National Association of Detox Acupuncture) with John Tindal at the Gateway Clinic, London.
    • Ongoing personal psychotherapeutic support and Cranial Osteopathic treatment with Robert Lever, one of the country’s pre-eminent practitioners.
    • Sports and fitness qualification from the Lucas Tooth Gymnasium.
